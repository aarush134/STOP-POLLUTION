Pollution Awareness

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment, affecting air, water, and land.

Causes of Pollution

Pollution is caused by industrial waste, vehicle emissions, deforestation, and improper waste disposal.

Effects of Pollution

It leads to climate change, health issues, loss of biodiversity, and damage to ecosystems.

How Can We Help?

Reduce waste, use eco-friendly products, plant trees, and support renewable energy.

Thank you for taking action!
